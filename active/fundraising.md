# Fundraising Working Group

## Scope of responsibilities

This working group coordinates fundraising efforts, particularly around corporate and major donations.

The goals of the group are:

- Acquire funding to secure the financial future of Django and support future growth of Django
- Build and strengthen relationships between Django and donor sources
- Raise awareness of the Django Web Framework and its community with the broader world

Delegated responsibilities:

- Contacting, developing and implementing financial sponsorships with donor sources
- Clarifying and elaborating on the various creative strategies that donor sources of different sizes and types can use to support Django
